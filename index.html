Influenza

Cause: RNA virus → Influenza A, B, C, D

Effects: upper respiratory distress

Common names: The Flu

Influenza

"The Flu" is specified as an A and B strain. Type A mutates very quickly due to cross-species infection.

Influenza

Most Flu victims get better within a week, but the disease causes an average of 36,000 deaths per year since 2010.
